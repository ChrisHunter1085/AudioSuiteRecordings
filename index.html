About Chris Hunter

Chris Hunter is a seasoned boutique audio engineer and artist manager with over 15 years of hands-on experience in the music and entertainment industry. From recording and mixing full-length projects to producing audiobooks and voiceovers, Chris has built a reputation for clean, dynamic sound and professional delivery. His engineering work spans hip-hop, R&B, and commercial sound, with releases featured on Spotify, Apple Music, and major streaming platforms.

As a manager, Chris has helped develop and guide independent artists through branding, project execution, and distribution—collaborating with talents like Translee, Grey, Mike MRTN, and more. Whether in the studio or behind the scenes, Chris brings technical skill, creative vision, and a deep respect for the craft.
